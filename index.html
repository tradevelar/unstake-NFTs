UNSTAKE NFTs

Admin tool. Grant operator roles, then force-unstake every NFT in a staking pool.

Configuration

Wallet

Not connected. VeWorld extension required.

Step 1 · Grant OPERATOR roles

Single multi-clause transaction. Grants OPERATOR_ROLE on the staking pool to both addresses above. Connected wallet must hold DEFAULT_ADMIN on the staking pool.

Step 2 · Unstake loop

Calls recoverERC721fromStaked(0, batch, staking, nft) on the recovery contract while remaining ≥ 50, then switches to per-token callExit(tokenId, staking) for the tail (avoids the off-by-one Panic 0x32 in the batch function).
